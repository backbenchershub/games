4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Where Your Data is Stored

Local Storage: Most of your gaming data (scores, progress, preferences) is stored locally in your browser using localStorage technology. This means your data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Data Location

  • Your Device: Game progress, scores, achievements, settings
  • Our Servers: Anonymous analytics, contact form submissions, error logs
  • Third-Party Services: CDN services for fonts and libraries (see section 8)

4.2 Security Measures

Technical Safeguards

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure server configurations
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Access controls and authentication

Operational Safeguards

  • Limited access to personal data
  • Regular security training for team members
  • Incident response procedures
  • Data minimization practices

7. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

7.1 What We Use

Technology Purpose Duration Can You Control It?
localStorage Save game progress and preferences Until you clear browser data Yes - clear browser storage
sessionStorage Temporary game session data Until browser tab is closed Yes - close browser tab
Analytics Cookies Understand platform usage 13 months Yes - browser settings or opt-out

7.2 Managing Cookies and Storage

Browser Controls

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Site Settings > Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions
Note: Disabling localStorage will prevent games from saving your progress. You can still play, but your scores and achievements won't be remembered between sessions.
