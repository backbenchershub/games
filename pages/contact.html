Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug in a game?
Please use the contact form above with "Bug Report" as the subject. Include details about which game, what device you're using, and steps to reproduce the issue.
Are the games mobile-friendly?
Yes! All our games are designed to work seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices with responsive controls and optimized layouts.
How does the level system work?
Our level system tracks your progress across all games. You earn XP by playing games, achieving high scores, and completing challenges. Your progress is saved locally in your browser.
Can I suggest new games or features?
Absolutely! We love hearing from our community. Use the contact form with "Feature Request" as the subject and tell us about your ideas.
Is there a community or forum?
We're working on building community features! For now, feel free to reach out to us directly with feedback, suggestions, or just to say hello.
