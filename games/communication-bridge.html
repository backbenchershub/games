How to Play Communication Bridge

Player 1 (Describer):

  • You will see an image in your display area
  • Describe the image in detail to Player 2
  • Be specific but don't use obvious giveaways
  • Click "Send Description" when done

Player 2 (Guesser):

  • Read the description from Player 1
  • Choose between two images
  • Click on the image you think matches the description

Scoring:

  • Correct guess: +1 point
  • Try to get as many correct guesses as possible
