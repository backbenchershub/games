How to Play Group Puzzle

Game Objective:

  • Work together to arrange the numbers from 1 to 9 in order
  • Numbers should be arranged from left to right, top to bottom

How to Move:

  • Click and drag pieces to swap their positions
  • Work with other players to decide the best moves
  • Try to complete the puzzle in as few moves as possible

Tips:

  • Focus on getting one row correct at a time
  • Communicate with your team about your next moves
  • Watch the timer and try to improve your speed
